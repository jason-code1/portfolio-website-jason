About JTC

JTC (Jad Touma Crafts) is a family-owned woodworking business, where a father and son team combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design. We specialize in creating custom furniture from premium Lamaika wood, crafting each piece to meet your unique needs and style preferences.

Every piece we create is made with passion, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality. We believe in the beauty of natural wood and the joy of handcrafted items that can be treasured for generations.
